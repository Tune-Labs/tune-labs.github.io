The Concept

Whether you are a producer without a MIDI keyboard or a songwriter striking inspiration on the go, Nada uses advanced pitch detection to turn your microphone into a versatile MIDI controller.

Voice to Melody

Hum, whistle, or sing to play pianos, synths, and guitars. Lock your voice to a specific Key and Scale so you don't hit a wrong note.

Voice to Beats

Turn beatboxing into tight drum loops. Teach the engine your sounds: "Boom" becomes a Kick, "Kak" becomes a Snare.

Multi-Track Studio

Layer your ideas into a full song. Arrange and mix multiple tracks with many instruments to experiment on.

Export & Share

Send melodies directly to your favorite DAW as standard MIDI files, or share raw audio recordings and rendered mixes.
